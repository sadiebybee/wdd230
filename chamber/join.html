Membership Levels

Non Profit

Cost: For non profit organizations and there is no fee.

Benefits: In the non-profit membership package, you will receive access to basic resources and information, you will receive a subscription to newsletters or updates, and you will receive an invitation to general events or meetings.

Bronze Membership

Cost: Slightly higher than the non-profit level.

Benefits: In the bronze membership package, you will receive all benefits of the non-profit level, additional access to certain training sessions and workshops, recognition on the organization's website and publications, and discounted rates for specific events and services.

Silver Membership

Cost: Slightly higher than the bronze level.

Benefits: In the silver membership package, you will receive all benefits of the bronze and non-profit memberships, priority registration for events and workshops, featured spotlight positions on the organization's website and social media, and enhanced networking opportunites, such as exclusive meet-and-greets.

Gold Membership

Cost: Slightly higher than the silver membership

Benefits: In the gold membership package, you will receive all benefits from all other membership packages, exclusive access to VIP events and private networking sessions, personalized advertising opportunites, such as dedicated email blasts or sponsored content, and special recognition at major events and in organizational materials.
